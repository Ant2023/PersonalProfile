About Me


Hello! I'm Anita, a fervent professional in the realm of technology and management, currently residing in Denver, CO. Originally from the picturesque landscapes of Zambia, my journey to the United States in 2012 marked the beginning of a new chapter in my life, filled with growth, discovery, and the pursuit of my passions.


My Professional Path


With over 5 years of experience as a Certified Scrum Master and Project Manager, I've honed my skills in navigating the complexities of Agile and Waterfall project delivery. My tenure at Charter Communications and ZICT-ZiCloudTech in Denver has not only solidified my expertise in scrum practices and project management but also in overcoming SDLC challenges. My educational pursuit in Computer Science at Colorado State University-Global, set to culminate in 2024, underscores my commitment to continual learning and excellence in web development.
Beyond my professional achievements, I'm an independent business owner with over a decade of experience. My entrepreneurial journey includes leading International Nightlife and Entertainment LLC, an event planning venture that celebrates diverse cultures and brings joy to the Colorado community. 303 Fitness Store stands as a testament to my dedication to health and wellness, while The New Face of Africa, a nonprofit organization, reflects my commitment to making a difference and connecting communities.
